Cookies policy

A cookie is a file that your browser downloads when you access some web pages. Cookies allow a website, among other things, store and retrieve information about browsing habits of a user and can be used to recognize the user. Browsers store cookies on your hard disk only during the current session using a minimum space of memory and they do not harm the computer. Cookies do not contain any specific personal information, and most of them are deleted from the hard drive at the end of the browser session (so-called session cookies).

Most browsers accept cookies as a standard and they allow via security settings to block them.
